Find Your Next Favourite Book!



Trying to decide what to read? Want to see what books are trending?

We’ve all been there.

If you want to find a new book but don’t want to register for accounts or create book lists. You’ve come to the right place. The Books You’ve Been Meaning to Read is a platform made for individuals who share a common interest: Reading.

This site allows you to browse various fictional genres and bestselling books and series without making an account and without creating book lists. Unlike most book recommendation sites The Books You’ve Been Meaning to Read directs you to lists that are not based on books you have already read.

This site is unique in that it provides you with the opportunity to explore several different genres, to select a specific genre that you’re interested in, and to scroll through lists of books that may be interesting to you.

Once you have found a genre or book that you find interesting you will be directed to an online bookstore where the genre can be explored and where the book can be purchased.

The Books You’ve Been Meaning to Read provides readers with non-personalized book recommendations, and it is a simple and free platform.

The Books You’ve Been Meaning to Read was developed specifically to help readers find a new book to read.

So, my fellow readers, browse for some new reading material, and welcome to The Books You’ve been Meaning to Read!

The Books You’ve Been Meaning to Read is a group of individuals who aim to help readers find their next book. This is not a real website; it was created for educational purposes only.
